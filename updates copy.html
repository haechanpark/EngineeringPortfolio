P1.2.7 Project Curcuit Design

October 12th, 2020- October 21st, 2020

Digital Electronics
For project 1.2.7 on circuit design, I had the objective and goal of developing and designing a circuit system with at least 3 inputs and 1 outputs with a defined purpose. I decided to replicate traffic light controls and make it so that in the case of emergencies such as natural disasters, the traffic lights could be individually controlled by someone in order to safely redirect traffic. As a solo project, I was responsible for creating documentation to log the project, design a circuit that fit the minimum constraints while also addressing the problem statement, and physically replicate the circuit from multisim to the breadboard.
